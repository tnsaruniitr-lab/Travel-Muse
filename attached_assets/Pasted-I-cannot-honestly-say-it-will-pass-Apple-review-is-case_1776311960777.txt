What Apple requires:

a public privacy policy URL in App Store Connect, and it must be a real policy page, not a placeholder; Apple says all apps must include the privacy policy link in App Store Connect metadata and within the app in an easily accessible way.
the policy must clearly state what data is collected, how it is collected, all uses of that data, third parties that receive it, and retention/deletion policy.
your App Privacy answers must include data collected by your app and third-party SDKs/partners, and they must stay accurate.
